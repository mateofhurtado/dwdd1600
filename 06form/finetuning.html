Fine Tuning Your Style

When it comes to having great style, knowing what types of colors, silhouettes and pieces you love and how they all fit together, is only part of the equation. The other part is having the confidence to actually wear those things.

I believe you can be into clothes and have fun with fashion without getting sucked into that vortex of societal pressure to fit in, follow current trends, look like a fitness model and wear perfectly polished outfits 24/7 like a fashion blogger.

SOURCE

Important things to think about when picking your style:

Color
The color you wear can really bring out the naturals colors that we see in your skin, hair, and eyes. Some colors are more slimming, and can even affect the mood that your outfit is giving off.
Pattern
Patterns also can have a slimming, or even widening effect on your body. You can use pattern in your style to visually change your shape or sometimes your height appearance.
Material
The material of clothes and accessories that you wear also change the mood you give off. Will you be going with more classic fabrics/materials, or something bold and new?
